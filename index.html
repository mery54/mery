Dominique Méry is professor of computing science at Université de Lorraine, where he teaches formal methods and related topics in the school of computer engineering Telecom Nancy and in the Faculty of Science and Technology. His research activities are developed in the laboratory LORIA joint structure of CNRS, INRIA and Université de Lorraine. You can contact him using the email of LORIA.
